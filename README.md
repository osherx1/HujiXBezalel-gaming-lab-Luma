## 🎮 About The Game

**Luma** is a fast-paced, two-player duel between the **Sun** and the **Moon**.  
Claim the center, collapse the ground beneath your rival, and don't lose your cool.

Jump across floating clouds, attack your opponent, and race to the finish line.  
**Shine brighter — or fall trying.**

## 🎯 How To Play

| Action | Control |
|:---|:---|
| **Move** | Left Stick / Arrow Keys |
| **Jump** | X (PlayStation: ▢) |
| **Lightning Attack** | Square (PlayStation: ▢) |

### Objective
Race your rival across the clouds and reach the finish line to score points.  
Use your **lightning attack** to knock the other player off the clouds — if they fall, they die and respawn!

### Winning
First player to fill all the slots on the finish line wins the game!

## 🧱 Project Structure

```
Assets/
├── Scripts/
│   ├── Player/          # Player movement, jumping, attack logic
│   ├── Cloud/           # Cloud movement & behavior
│   ├── FinishLine/      # Finish slot scoring system
│   ├── Managers/        # GameManager, UIManager, SoundManager
│   ├── UI/              # Screen transitions & fade effects
│   ├── Camera/          # Camera shake & effects
│   ├── Sky/             # Background & sky system
│   └── Enums/           # TeamType, AudioType, etc.
├── Scenes/              # Unity scenes (GameLevel, Win screens)
├── Images/              # Character art, backgrounds, UI elements
├── Sprites/             # Sun & Moon sprites, references
├── Audio/               # Music & sound effects
├── Animations/          # Character animations (Happy, Sad, Surprised)
├── Prefabs/             # Reusable game objects
└── Settings/            # Render pipeline & input settings
```
